Things I Like!

Gaming

like to play games on pc. I have been doing it since I was 5 years old. My dad used to be a computer engineer, so my brothers and I was introduced to computers at an early age. At the time (around 1997+), I played games like Doom, Quake, Duke Nukem3D, Halflife 1 and Diablo 1. I played these games Mostly because these were the games installed on my brothers computers, and we switched who got to play.

When I was 10 my parents got me my first computer, so that i could join my brother on LAN. These were small gatherings in the weekends, between my brother and his friends who are 4 years older than me. Today i play a variaty of single and multiplayer games, and its a great way to stay connected with old friends who have moved far away.

Piano

I like playing the piano. I am not very good at it, but it is still fun. I tried to learn from a teacher for a few months, but I prefer to watch/listen and repeat from youtube or midi programs.

Cooking

I enjoy cooking from scratch and learning new recipes.

Watching TV

Binging tv shows has become one of my favorite things to do. I remember I would go with my family to a shop and rent movies, thankfully, with the emergence of streaming services, it's just a click away.

One of my favourite shows is Critical Role and I watch the vods on YouTube. It's about a couple of voice actors who stream while they play DnD together once a week. Each session lasts 4 hours.

Art

I used to draw all the time and did art classes in upper secondary school. I doodle now and again but haven't drawn in a long time.

Table Top

I enjoy playing board games with friends. Back in Norway, some friends and I used to get together every other month, eat good food and play board games while having drinks and snacks. We usually play Betrayal at house on the hill or Gloomhaven.

Crafting

I like to craft when I get the opportunity. I recently made a dice tower that looks like a haunted house for our board game and also painted the figurines.
